Welcome to This Magic Moment Events!

My name is Annie Stephanie Agopian and I have worked with creative professionals throughout my entire career.

With over five years of event planning and coordinating experience and working alongside celebrity stylist and event planner, Anya Sarre, I have become enthralled with watching people create memorable experiences.

I look forward to the opportunity to work with you!
